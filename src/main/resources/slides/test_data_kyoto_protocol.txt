Kyoto Protocol :-
Ø A un- brokered treaty was signed by in 1997 in Kyoto Japan. It was called the Kyoto Protocol.
Ø 
Ø  It commits the rich countries to binding cut in their emission of greenhouse gases, such as carbon dioxide.
Ø 
Ø The developed countries agreed to cut their emissions by a collective average of 5 percent below their levels in 1990 by 2008-2012.
Ø 
Ø  Representative Climate Conference (COPC) in Nov 2000, and discussed about the implementation of the Kyoto Protocol.
